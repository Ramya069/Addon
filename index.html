What is the Netflix offer with jio prepaid?
Jio is offering Netflix (Mobile Plan) subscription to its JioPostPaid Plus customers on select JioPostpaid plans at no extra cost. With Netflix, customers can enjoy unlimited movies and TV shows from Hollywood to Bollywood, Indian regional movies and popular TV shows, anytime, anywhere on their mobile devices.
How can I watch Netflix after taking the Netflix offer?
Netflix has an extensive global and local content library featuring award-winning Netflix originals, feature films, documentaries, TV shows, and more. The more you watch, the better Netflix gets at recommending TV shows and movies you’ll love. You can play, pause, and resume watching without ads or commitments. Plus, you can download your favorite shows to your iOS or Android mobile device. With downloads, you can watch while you're on the go and without an internet connection.
